What is Bitrix24

What is Bitrix24

Bitrix24 CRM is a powerful customer relationship management and sales automation system. This is a corporate portal that combines project management, tasks, website builder, integration with social networks.networks and end-to-end analytics.

Calls and correspondence with customers, stages of sales and transactions, storage of documentation and reports — all collected in one place!

Bitrix24 integration is the introduction of an automated sales management system into the business processes of an organization.

This is an opportunity to start flexibly and effectively managing employee time and organization resources.

Our company is professionally engaged in the integration and configuration of CRM Bitrix24. We will be happy to show you all the features of the program and draw up a detailed plan for its implementation in your company's activities.

Bitrix24 integration works include support and training of employees at all stages of implementation.

Features CRM Bitrix24

Features CRM Bitrix24

If there is any way to improve business processes, then it is safe to say that Bitrix24 is already using it!

The program includes hundreds of modern tools for doing business, obtaining conversion statistics and forecasting sales. A virtual workplace has been implemented: you can do business directly from your smartphone. It is convenient to communicate with colleagues: built—in online chat, video conferences will help you connect your manager to a deal at any time.

Almost all processes can now be automated: document templates, robots, triggers, reports and built-in analytics are available. Get conversion statistics, predict the probability of a deal – with Bitrix24, all this will take a couple of seconds.

Advantages of CRM Bitrix24 integration

It is important for the manager to know how effectively the business is working. That's what Bitrix24 is for: it connects all the links of the company into a single mechanism. Moreover, the built-in tools of the system allow you to identify weaknesses in the workflow and take timely measures to improve the efficiency of the business.

By integrating Bitrix24 into your company's business processes, you will receive:

Automation of all stages of sales — all applications and leads are immediately sent to your company's CRM. Phone calls are made directly from the program interface and recorded in the client's card. You will know everything about your customers!

End-to-end analytics that will show all the points of contact between customers and your company. It will show you how your sales funnel works and how it can be set up even better. It will generate reports that are important for business development.

A convenient virtual environment for communication with customers and communications within the company. Sending forms and documents, completing training, holding conferences and meetings — powerful functionality will allow managers to work as efficiently as possible.

Optimization of all business processes: Bitrix24 will turn repetitive operations into scripts, templates and triggers. This will free up a huge amount of working time and reduce the cost of office work.

Website builder. Now you can have as many sites as you need for high-quality work. You can create and customize a website for each task or product.

Budget savings. The program itself will give you signals about the quality of employees' work, the upcoming deadlines for completing tasks and the effectiveness of advertising companies.

Questions about Integration CRM Bitrix24

What is the main functionality of Bitrix24 CRM?
The Bitrix24 portal is a unique platform where all the most necessary modern tools are collected in one place, ensuring high quality customer service.The functionality includes the following sections:

CRM is a powerful customer relationship management unit. No contact with the client will now be lost. The program records the history of all types of customer interactions in its storage, tells you what to do next and automates sales by sending offers of promotions and discounts to customers.

Tasks and Projects are a block with which you can build professional project management. Create tasks, assign responsible persons and monitor the success of their implementation. Timely notification of the date of completion of the task will reduce by sending offers of promotions and discounts to customers.

The contact center is a single information space that combines all communication channels of the company's employees with each other and with their customers. Online chat, live feed, social networks, messengers - all information gets into the client's card and is assigned to the responsible employee.

Built-in website builder. It allows you to quickly create beautiful, modern websites using a large set of template solutions, from a one–page website and a corporate portal, to a full-fledged online store with a large range of goods and services.
